Welcome! I’m Audrey Rentz

Hi, I’m Audrey Rentz, a Computer Science student at BYU-I with a passion for combining logic and creativity! While working in office roles at an engineering company, I was introduced to software engineering and web development and became fascinated by the world of coding. This exposure piqued my curiosity and led me to pursue a degree in Computer Science.

Blending Creativity & Technology

I love the challenge of problem-solving through code, and I’m currently learning Python, HTML, and CSS as I build my foundation in programming. I also have a strong creative side and love an artistic outlet. My recent outlets include watercolor, embroidery, and pottery. I love to try new things and consider myself to be a life-long learner.

I am currently in my first semester of Computer Science. As I continue my studies, I look forward to developing my coding skills, exploring web development, and discovering ways to blend design and technology. This website is my portfolio and learning space, where I’ll share projects, ideas, and creative work.

Let’s Connect!

Feel free to explore my portfolio to see what I’m working on. I’m always excited to learn new things and collaborate with others who share a passion for creativity and technology!
