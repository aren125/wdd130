Sampson Junior Leadership Program

Organization: Clinton-Sampson Chamber of Commerce

This initiative was designed to equip local high school students with leadership skills, community engagement opportunities, and career development resources.

My Role & Contributions

  • Designed and structured the curriculum and program schedule.
  • Organized guest speakers, workshops, and networking events.
  • Coordinated partnerships with local businesses and community leaders.
  • Facilitated leadership activities to help students develop communication, teamwork, and problem-solving skills.
